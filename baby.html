Baby

Materials: Watercolor and marker on paper
Year: 2023
Dimensions: 29.7 × 42 cm
Status: Not available for purchase

This piece represents the generation of an actual human baby (yellow/orange energy ball). The parents of the baby (blue and purple energy balls) produce the baby. In this fascinating process, the parents lose parts of themselves, entangle with each other, and synthesize a new human being. This piece was a gift for a dear friend of mine and I finalized this piece long before the baby was conceived.

I believe that, as souls, we choose our parents before we incarnate on earth. We learn from them and teach them lessons just by existing.
