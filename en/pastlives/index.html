Past Lives

A collaborative art universe in chapters: image, sound, story.

Purpose

To soften the fear of death by exploring past lives as part of our journey as souls.

The concept

What if death is returning home? What if we come here in chapters? Past Lives is a collection of these chapters: remembered, imagined, archetypal, or discovered through dreams and hypnotherapy.
