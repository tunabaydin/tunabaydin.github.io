Coming down

Materials: Marker on paper
Year: 2023
Dimensions: 21 × 30 cm
Status: Available for purchase

I went on a trip with my close friends. Once the journey was completed and we were returning home, I found a moment to create this piece.

There is something in experiencing an event in the moment and looking back at it from the future once it already happened. As human beings in 3D, we perceive time as linear. I believe, as higher beings, we do not experience a linear time. There, the past, present and future exist all at once.

In the piece, the borders of shapes follow an irregular pattern. So you can decide what the foreground is and where the background starts.
