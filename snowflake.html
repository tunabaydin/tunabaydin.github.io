Snowflake

Chocolate wrapping paper · 2024 · 415 x 765 mm

The main inspiration for the art was repurposing the chocolate packaging. There are 2 components to the packaging, a hard colorful paper and a thinner shiny paper, both containing patterns. I also used a black marker and glue to put the pieces together.

There are 5 different patterns which corresponds to 5 different flavors of chocolate. They all form an individual "family" within. Being opposite to each other, symbolizing 2 parents we inherit genetic (and sometimes cultural) material from. They all originate from the same source and form a unique mandala style pattern. Just like a unique snowflake.
